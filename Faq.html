Most frequent asked questions

Q.How do you define a classic vechicle?

A. We define a classic vehicle as being over 20 years old, but we are always flexible if you drive something you love, and which we think our visitors would like to see and welcome you to join our club and explore the journey together with together.

Q.Can i drive vintage cars?

A.If you have a permanent driving licence and a clean credit score, you can become a member of Vintage rally car Club. Our cars can only be driven by members: so make sure you registered.

Q. Do we need condition to drive a vintage car?

A.You need a valid, permanent driving licence and a clean credit score.

Q. Can i bring my own vechiles?

A.We welcome you to participate and show other what your car made of.

Q.How much will the rally cost?

A. Entrance Fee – The cost to enter either of our car rallies in 2021 is $50 for a team of two people. If you wish to add persons to help reduce the costs, we ask for another $25 per person.
