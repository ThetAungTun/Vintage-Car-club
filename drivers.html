Members

Comprised of 241 motoring and motor sport club members in 146 countries, the vintage rally car club is in a unique position to facilitate communication and the exchange of ideas among its members. This comprehensive access means that the FIA and its clubs can actively educate and inform members of their responsibilities and encourage them to behave safely and make choices that are environmentally sound.
